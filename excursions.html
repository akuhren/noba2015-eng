EXCURSIONS

Sunday, July 5 at 11.15 a.m.

Bus departure from Thisted Gymnasium to the Lithuanian monuments at the outskirts of Thisted.

The monument was originally built by Lituanian refugees i 147 after their stay in Thisted after the second world war.

At 12.00 we continue to Ashøje close to Hurup where we enjoy a light lunch before the performance of  Fyrspillene – “The three ringings”.

At 4.15 p.m. we return to Thisted where we are expected to arrive at 5 p.m.





Tuesday, July 7 at 9.30 a.m.

Excursion with bus departure from Thisted Gymnasium at 9.30 a.m.

We will drive through the first and biggest Danish national park – Nationalpark Thy – see the world’s biggest test center for wind mills, take the ferry to the island of Mors and go the west coast of Thy.

At 6.00 p.m. we arrive at the manor Rosvang, where we have a dinner before seeing the performance of Kulturforeningen Rosvand – “Double murder at Rosvang”. We expect to be back at Thisted Gymnasium at 10 p.m.
